TRACE (Tracking, Retrieving, and Auditing for Coherent Epics) supports long-form writing by reducing the cognitive load of maintaining narrative consistency across millions of words.

We propose a “Write, Audit, Correct” workflow for author-controlled human-AI collaboration, and build a system leveraging dynamic structured memory to detect potential narrative inconsistencies while preserving authors’ creative flow.
